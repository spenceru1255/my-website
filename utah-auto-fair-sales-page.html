The process

How Utah Auto Fair works.

Three simple steps: pre-register, bring your car, and take buyer calls or offers directly.

Step 01

Submit your vehicle

Fill out the registration form with your vehicle details. We review submissions so the lot is organized and useful for buyers.

Step 02

Bring it to the event lot

Arrive during seller check-in, park in your assigned spot, and place your seller information where buyers can easily understand your vehicle.

Step 03

Talk directly with buyers

Interested buyers contact you directly. You negotiate the sale, handle payment, complete paperwork, and keep every dollar.

Utah seller facts

Helpful Utah paperwork reminders.

This section replaces people photos with useful, Utah-specific information. Always confirm current rules with the Utah DMV before completing a sale.

🧾

Title and registration

Utah DMV seller guidance says to give the new owner the signed title, current registration, and a lien release if applicable.

🔁

Online title transfer option

Utah DMV offers a Person to Person Online Title Transfer service for certain Utah-to-Utah transactions.

🚗

License plate reminder

Utah DMV says sellers should remove the license plate unless the plates are being sold with the vehicle.
